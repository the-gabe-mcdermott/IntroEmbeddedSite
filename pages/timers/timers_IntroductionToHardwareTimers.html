Introduction to Hardware Timers

What Is A Hardware Timer?

A hardware timer, or often simply "timer" is a circuit used to accuratly measure the passage of time. Some timers are external chips you can wire up to a microcontroller. Most microcontrollers however, contain timers built into them, and are acccessable via special hardware addresses.
